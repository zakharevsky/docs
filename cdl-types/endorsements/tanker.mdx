## What is the Tanker Vehicles Endorsement?
A tanker endorsement, represented by the letter "N" on a CDL, is an additional qualification that permits drivers to transport liquid and gas in bulk. This endorsement is essential for drivers who plan to haul a variety of food-grade materials, such as:
  - Water
  - Milk
  - Juice
  - Liquid sugar
  - Vegetable oil
  - Vinegar

The endorsement ensures that drivers are equipped with the knowledge and skills required to safely handle and transport these types of loads, which often have unique handling and safety requirements.


<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why Get a Tanker Endorsement?">
    This endorsement is crucial for drivers who want to work in sectors that involve the transport of bulk liquids or gases, which require specialized knowledge due to their potentially hazardous nature.
  </Accordion>

<Accordion title="When is a Tanker Endorsement Required?">
You need this endorsement when driving a commercial vehicle designed to transport 1,000 gallons or more of liquid or gas. This includes vehicles that aren't always carrying loads but have the capacity to do so.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to Get a Tanker Endorsement on a CDL
The journey starts with studying. You need to understand the specifics of handling large volumes of liquid or gas. After studying, you'll take a knowledge test specific to tanker operations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pass the Tanker Knowledge Test">
    Start with CDL training focused on tanker operations. Then, study for and pass a [tanker endorsement knowledge (written) test](/essentials/written-tests/tankers) focused on the safe operation of tank vehicles, including handling different types of liquids and understanding the physics of liquid movement. 
  </Step>

  <Step title="Meet Additional Requirements">
    Depending on your state, there might be additional requirements or endorsements needed, such as the HazMat endorsement for hazardous liquids.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding the Requirements

**How Many Gallons Require a Tanker Endorsement?**
- Generally, if you're transporting liquids or gases in a volume of 1,000 gallons or more, a tanker endorsement is a must.

**How Many Gallons of Water Can You Haul Without a Tanker Endorsement?**
- You can haul less than 1,000 gallons of water without needing a tanker endorsement. But once you hit that threshold, the endorsement becomes mandatory.
