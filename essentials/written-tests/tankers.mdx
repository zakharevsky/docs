If you're aiming to become a tanker truck driver, the CDL Tanker Endorsement Test is a crucial step. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to pass the test and secure your endorsement.

## What is the CDL Tanker Endorsement Test?

The CDL Tanker Endorsement Test assesses your knowledge and skills related to safely transporting liquid or gaseous materials in bulk.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Who Needs a Tanker Endorsement?" icon="question">
    Any CDL holder who plans to drive a commercial tank vehicle needs to pass this endorsement test.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Tank Vehicle Definition" icon="truck-moving">
    A tank vehicle is defined as any commercial vehicle that is designed to transport any liquid or gaseous materials within a tank.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

| Exam Feature           | Details                                           |
|------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| Number of Questions    | Usually 20-30 multiple-choice                     |
| Passing Score          | Typically 80% or higher                           |
| Time Limit             | Varies by state; often around 30-60 minutes       |
| Key Topics             | Bulk tank operations, inspecting tank vehicles, driving tank vehicles, handling liquids and gases, emergency procedures |
| Test Format            | Computer-based in most states                     |
| Retake Policy          | Varies by state; may include waiting periods and additional fees for retakes |
| Prerequisite           | Valid CDL; specific requirements may vary by state |

</Steps>
